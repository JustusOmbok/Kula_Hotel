About Kula Hotel

Overview

Welcome to Kula Hotel, where luxury meets comfort. We are committed to providing an exceptional experience for both guests and staff. Our web application seamlessly integrates various features to streamline hotel management and enhance the user experience.

Our Mission

At Kula Hotel, our mission is to redefine hospitality by combining cutting-edge technology with warm, personalized service. We aim to create an environment where guests feel at home and where staff can efficiently manage hotel operations.

Key Features

  • Room Management: Easily add new rooms, update details, and delete outdated information.
  • Booking System: Streamlined booking process with dynamic pricing and availability checks.
  • User Authentication: Secure login and registration for both guests and staff.
  • Dashboard Functionality: Intuitive dashboards for users and staff, providing quick access to relevant actions.
  • Data Retrieval: Retrieve and display booking details effortlessly, whether for specific IDs or all bookings.
  • Update Bookings: Modify booking details, including check-in and check-out dates.
  • User Management: Efficiently handle user registrations and logins, ensuring a secure and seamless experience.

Kula Royal Hotel View
Nairobi, Kenya

Kula Royal Hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, a garden and terrace in Nairobi. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service, and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The property is 2.9 km from the city centre and 5.9 km from Nairobi Railway Station.

Standard Suite

Features include: a queen-sized bed with crisp linens, an en-suite bathroom with toiletries, a dresser, a desk with chairs, a flat-screen TV, climate control, and Wi-Fi access.
